App Management Made Easy

Distributing enterprise apps to the right device and users is a major challenge of enterprise mobility today. Far too often IT departments develop powerful productivity improving apps but lack an effective mechanism to distribute those apps. The apps are not used and innovation is subdued. RhoGallery changes all that. It gives IT the ability to deliver applications and updates when they want and to whoever they want across multiple operating system and devices.

Delivering Apps the Enterprise way

Return application delivery and update controls to the experts—IT. RhoGallery lets administrators deliver applications in an App Store experience directly to their users allowing IT to controls apps, versions and users. Immediately deliver the apps you need when you need to deliver them.

Increase App Usage, Increase Worker Productivity

Enabling direct app delivery provides employees with a simple solution to download and install those applications without having to follow URLs to a website or find applications in stores. The simplicity increases usage and adoption of your Enterprise apps.

App Reviews and Ratings

Get immediate feedback from your Enterprise App users. RhoGallery enable users to rate your applications and provide immediate feedback. Know what your users think and increase their satisfaction.
