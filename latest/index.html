Open Source and Massive, Mature Community

RhoElements is built on the free open source Rhodes framework. It merely adds several enterprise relevant capabilities such as barcoding and advanced security. Rhodes is the most powerful free framework, offering MVC and ORM and many other features not even conceived of by consumer frameworks. Rhodes was started in August 2008 as the first smartphone framework, and has maintained its lead in features over the last six years. Here are just some of the thousands of RhoMobile Suite apps.
