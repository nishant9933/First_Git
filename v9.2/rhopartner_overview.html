Partner Overview

The Rhomobile Partner Program is comprised of two partnership tiers: Certified Rhodes Partners and Rhomobile Elite Partners. Both Certified and Elite partners must pass a certification process which includes a technical and a sales training. All partners begin as Certified Rhodes Partners and with time and a proven track record, move on to become Rhomobile Elite Partners.

Focus

We are committed to helping our partners create success in the enterprise mobility space. We seek to partner with innovative systems integrators to deliver complete mobile solutions to large enterprises. With our “Write Once, Run Everywhere” Rhodes Framework, we work with our partners to increase their clients’ productivity, performance, and portability when it comes to smartphone apps. At Rhomobile, when our partners succeed, we succeed, and we are dedicated to providing all the support, benefits, and other assistance necessary for our partners to achieve success.
